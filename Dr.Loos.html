The Brief

Working with clientele Dr. Loo an expert in Alternative Medicine and CEO of Dr.Loo's Natural Remedies, based in San Jose, California. It was an honor to help shape and create colorful graphics, cohesive labels, and provide digital marketing experiences.

By providing exceptional product Designthat is safe and ergonomic, These Harmony Patches are made with natural and organic ingredients to soothe tummy aches and coughs of all ages. Dr. Loo's hollistic healing patches are waterbased. Designing the branding in tune with all natural ingredients include elements such as leafy patterns and soft pastels to match the "gentle" healing nature of Dr. Loo's products.
