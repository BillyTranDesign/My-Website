The Brief

HappyHam tea was designed to elevate the boba experience by joining the bubble tea revolution! Project HappyHam Tea utilized multiple processes to achieve its branding. Original studio photography was shot utilizing colorful boba drinks with the branding front and center. The logo Designprocess was inspired by a pet hamster bringing much joy in my life, in hopes this character in conjunction with boba brings as much happiness to all.

The final product was to create a editorial of the product a studio setting titled "The Boba Insider", by amalgamating Designand photographic practices, mockups and a logo were designed in the process of the project.
