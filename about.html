Hello!

My name is Billy Tran, a graduate at San Jose State University with a BA Design Studies, Concetration in Graphic Design. I was born in New York and currently reside in the Bay Area. Art, design, and photography have always been a passion of mine since I was young and now, pursuing within the creative field is a step closer to my dream!

Growing up as a Vietnamese-American, the beautiful Vietnamese culture has always been a part of my life which I draw inspiration from, also from my family, friends, anywhere. When I'm not at my desk, I love to cook and create new recipes and make spontaneous trips. I am also a content creator on the side, in hopes of bringing communities together while designing my own space on the platform. I enjoy problem-solving and finding creative solutions that can further reinforce my experiences as a designer and as a person; to design work worth doing...


Being able to continously provide high quality, fluid work that not only entices my clients but integrates my personal flair. Connecting society through my designs and photos to bring happiness to everyone.
