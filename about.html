Hello!

My name is Billy Tran, a graduate at San Jose State University with a BA Design Studies, concentration in Graphic Design. I was born in New York and currently reside in the Bay Area. Design, illustration and photography have always been a passion of mine since I was young and now, pursuing within my endeavors my dream has come true!

Growing up as a Vietnamese-American, the beautiful Vietnamese culture has always been a part of my life which I draw inspiration from, also from my family, friends, anywhere. When I'm not at my desk, I love to cook and create new recipes and make spontaneous trips. I am also a content creator on the side, in hopes of bringing communities together while designing my own space on the platform. I enjoy problem-solving and finding creative solutions that can further reinforce my experiences as a designer and as a person; to design work worth doing...

Being able to continously provide high quality, fluid work that not only entices my clients but integrates my personal flair. Connecting society through my designs and photos to bring happiness to everyone. Designing outside the box so you don't have to!

I'd love to connect with you and hopefully work on an amazing project together!
