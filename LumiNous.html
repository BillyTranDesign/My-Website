The Brief

All natural, beautiful you. Peachy Keen by Luminous, a conceptual product design project was designed with simplicity and organic ingredients in mind By utilizing both practices within photography and design, the intention of this project was to demonstrate the simplicity in CPG and how careful photographic production plays a pivotal role in conveying the product & brand. Going beyond the parameters of the project, the brandmark was established.

The final product showcase integrates design elements and studio photography practices to successfully execute a fully fleshed concept for a consumer product.
