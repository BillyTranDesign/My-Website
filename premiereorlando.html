About this collaboration

elan beaute is an lash extension & supplies company focused on uplifting and providing a safe space for lash artist alike, they're a renowned for supplying high-quality products for both professional lash artists and everyday consumers. For this collective project, elan had a booth set up at premiere orlando beauty convention depicting a vintage laundromat style theme.

Inspirations from real laundry care products and retro 90's cartoon characters were pivotal in the process for the pop-up. Characters such as Jessica Rabbit, Marilyn Monroe, Brats & rubberhose characters were designed on every product showcased. features a deep denim wash blue color pallete with kraft paper textures to give it the feel of a retro laundromat experience. Through this project, elan was able to network with bigger companies in the beauty industry, even leading to potential retail & wholesale potential.
