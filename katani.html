The Brief

Katani Orlando hosts and organizes special anime themed boba conventions with boba tea concoctions inspired by characters and elements from shows. They also outreach to local vendors to sell crafts of their own related to the theme of the event. Through my experience working with Katani Orlando, their brandmark and fundamental cupsleeves were created. Instagram was also utilized as a digital marketing strategy as a local event but quickly grew 2,000 followers through two events hosted thus far. Katani Orlando is currently planning a new event with future cupsleeve designs to be unveiled soon!
